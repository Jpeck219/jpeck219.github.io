Our Story

From Tripoli to a New Home

The Sasi family traces its roots to the Jewish community of Tripoli, Libya — a community with centuries of history, rich traditions, and a deep sense of belonging. In 1949, Behor and Chana Sasi made the courageous journey to Israel, carrying with them their heritage, their faith, and a vision for the generations to come.

This site is a living gathering place for the family they built — a space to share memories, celebrate milestones, and stay connected no matter where in the world we are.
